Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

EONS Animation Studio ("EONS," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website eons.studio and the EONS AI tool at eons.studio/studio-ai.html. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — When you fill out the contact form, we collect your name, email address, company name, and project details. When you use EONS AI, we collect your name, email, and company (optional) during the onboarding gate.
  • Usage data — We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized information about how visitors use our site, including pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Conversation data — AI conversations conducted through EONS AI are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We may receive a summary when you submit a concept to our team via the "Send to EONS" feature.
  • Cookies — We use essential cookies to remember your preferences (such as cookie consent and theme settings). Analytics cookies are only placed if you accept our cookie notice.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to project inquiries and schedule strategy calls
  • To understand how our website is used and improve it
  • To send your AI-generated brief to our team when you request it
  • To remember your preferences across visits

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics — Anonymized site usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • EmailJS — Used to send contact form submissions to our team email. Your message is transmitted but not stored by EmailJS beyond delivery.
  • Anthropic Claude API — Powers the EONS AI chat tool. Conversations may be processed by Anthropic per their Privacy Policy.
  • Vimeo — Used to host and stream portfolio video content. Subject to Vimeo's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Contact form submissions are delivered to our team email and not stored in a database. EONS AI conversation history is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it does not leave your device unless you choose to submit a concept to our team.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any personal information you have provided to us
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by declining cookies or using a browser extension such as uBlock Origin
  • Clear your EONS AI conversation history at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
