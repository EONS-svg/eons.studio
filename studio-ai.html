This is what the AI actually generates — not a mockup

Project Objective

Launch a 30-second hero video for a vegan lip gloss brand targeting Gen Z women aged 18-26. The goal is to drive direct-to-consumer conversions from paid social — specifically TikTok and Instagram Reels — with a secondary use case as a paid ad creative.

Target Audience

She's 22, aesthetics-driven, shops DTC brands she discovers on TikTok. She believes beauty should be clean and intentional — not performative. She scrolls fast. You have 1.5 seconds to earn her attention.

Brand Tone

Clean. Confident. Sensory. NOT clinical, NOT maximalist, NOT aspirational in a distant way. The brand should feel like something she already owns.

Visual Style Direction

Macro product hero shot — tight on the applicator pulling away from the tube, revealing a liquid gloss strand in slow motion. Subsurface scattering on the gloss material to show translucency. Warm studio lighting with a single rim light for product edge definition.

Recommended Package

Foundation — $2,500+ — This scope needs a hero asset plus vertical cut-downs. The 2-3 week timeline fits the detail level required on the macro liquid simulation.

Scene 01 — 0:00–0:03 · SIMPLE

Shot: Extreme close-up macro. Black void. Slow push-in toward product.
Animation: Product materializes via particle dissolution. Camera slowly pushes in at 0.2x speed.
Light: Single key light from 45° above right. No fill. Deep shadows.
Sound: Silence, then a single low ambient tone fades in.

Scene 02 — 0:03–0:10 · COMPLEX

Shot: Macro hero. Applicator pulls away from tube.
Animation: Liquid simulation — gloss strand stretches and breaks in ultra slow motion at 120fps. Subsurface scattering active on gloss material showing translucency and depth.
Light: Three-point studio. Rim light on gloss strand to catch the break moment.
Sound: Subtle liquid texture sound designed, pitched down.

Scene 03 — 0:10–0:18 · MODERATE

Shot: Medium product shot. Tube floating. Slow orbit clockwise.
Animation: Camera orbits 180°. Product rotates opposite direction at 0.5x speed. Color name fades in via opacity — Vulnerable.
Light: Soft environmental HDRI. Single accent light from below for brand warmth.
Sound: Music enters — slow tempo, 72bpm, warm synth.

Color Temperature

Warm neutral. 5200K. Not clinical white, not golden hour. Think clean studio with personality. The product colors should be the hero — everything else supports them.

Animation Pace

Slow burn. 4-6 cuts per minute maximum. Each cut is earned. This isn't a fast-paced ad — it's a moment. The viewer should feel like they've been given permission to look closely.

Music Direction

Reference: Caroline Polachek — Sunset (instrumental section), or FKA Twigs — Cellophane (stripped). Warm, minimal, slightly melancholic. 70-80bpm. No drums until the final reveal.

What To Avoid

No confetti particles. No rainbow prismatic lens flares. No fast-cut energy. No stock music. Nothing that looks like every other DTC beauty ad from 2021. This brand is quieter and more confident than that.
