// Common Questions

Frequently
Asked

Every project starts with a free strategy session — either through EONS AI or directly with Ryan. We align on the brief, visual direction, and timeline before any production begins. From there: concept development → storyboard approval → 3D production → revisions → final delivery. You're never in the dark — we share progress at every stage.

Pilot and Foundation packages include 2 revision rounds. Growth includes 3. Authority includes unlimited revisions within scope. We find that most projects are resolved well within these because our brief process front-loads alignment — we rarely arrive at delivery with surprises.

Yes — full ownership transfers to you upon final payment. You receive the rendered deliverables in all requested formats. Source files (Cinema 4D, Blender, After Effects) can be included in Growth and Authority packages upon request.

The more context the better — brand guidelines, product references, competitor examples, and a sense of where this will live (social, landing page, pitch deck). If you don't have all of this yet, EONS AI can help you build a brief before we even get on a call. That's what it's there for.

We offer an AI-Accelerated production track on all packages that compresses timelines by 30–40% using AI for storyboarding, blocking, and iteration phases while maintaining the same strategic oversight and final quality. Tell us your deadline upfront and we'll tell you honestly if we can hit it.

Standard delivery includes MP4 (H.264) optimized for web, plus platform-specific cuts for Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok where applicable. We can also provide ProRes for broadcast, transparent-background MOV for overlays, and still frames for thumbnails or ad creative.

Our Pilot package was designed specifically for early-stage teams that need to prove brand quality before a raise or launch. If you have a clear brief and budget, we can work with you. The EONS AI tool is free and can help you shape your concept before committing to a project.

We compete with boutique strategy-led motion studios, not freelancers or template agencies. The difference is judgment — we push back when a direction won't serve the business goal, bring strategic thinking to the brief, and build visual systems that compound over time rather than one-off videos. The result is brand equity, not just content.
