// About EONS

A Studio Built
For Growth.

EONS started in 2023 in New York City with a simple conviction: a brand grows fastest when its systems and its story move together. Too many businesses have great products that nobody sees, or great visibility built on operations held together with tape.

So we became two studios in one. On one side, we build AI systems — automations, assistants, and tooling that let small teams scale without scaling headcount. On the other, we produce 3D animation and motion that makes brands impossible to scroll past.

We're small, senior, and strategy-led. We push back when a direction won't serve the business goal, and we build things designed to compound — not one-off deliverables, but infrastructure and visual systems that keep working long after we ship.
